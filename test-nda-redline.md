# NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
### REDLINE — Reviewed against Vendor NDA Playbook | 2026-03-19

---

**This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement")** is entered into on this 19th day of March, 2026, by and between **OmniCorp Global Holdings LLC** ("Disclosing Party") and **Unfortunate Soul** ("Receiving Party").

---

### 1. Definition of Confidential Information

~~"Confidential Information" shall mean absolutely everything. This includes, but is not limited to, trade secrets, business plans, financial data, the weather outside the Disclosing Party's office, information already in the public domain, information the Receiving Party knew prior to signing this Agreement, and any thoughts or concepts the Receiving Party might independently develop while asleep.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** "Confidential Information" shall mean any non-public information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether orally, in writing, or by any other means, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure. Confidential Information shall **not** include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records; (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information; or (d) is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.

> **Playbook Note:** Original definition captured public domain information, prior knowledge, and independently developed concepts — all standard exclusions that must be restored.

---

### 2. ~~Lack of Exceptions~~ **[RETITLE: Compelled Disclosure]**

~~The Receiving Party agrees that there are **no exceptions** to the definition of Confidential Information. If the Receiving Party is ordered by a court of competent jurisdiction or a government agency to disclose the Confidential Information, the Receiving Party agrees to choose imprisonment or contempt of court rather than disclose said information.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** If the Receiving Party is required by applicable law, regulation, or a valid order of a court or government authority to disclose any Confidential Information, the Receiving Party shall: (a) provide the Disclosing Party with prompt prior written notice of such requirement to the extent permitted by law; (b) cooperate with the Disclosing Party in seeking a protective order or other appropriate remedy; and (c) disclose only that portion of the Confidential Information that is legally required to be disclosed.

> **Playbook Note:** Prohibiting compelled disclosure is unenforceable and exposes the Receiving Party to contempt liability. Standard carve-out required.

---

### 3. Term and ~~Perpetual~~ **[AMENDED]** Confidentiality

~~The obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall never expire. The Receiving Party agrees to maintain **Perpetual Confidentiality**. This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement, the termination of employment, the Receiving Party's natural lifespan, and the eventual heat death of the universe. The Receiving Party's heirs, descendants, and future pets are also permanently bound by these terms.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** The obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **five (5) years** from the date of disclosure of the applicable Confidential Information. Notwithstanding the foregoing, obligations with respect to information that constitutes a trade secret under applicable law shall survive until such information no longer qualifies as a trade secret. This Agreement shall bind only the parties hereto and their permitted successors and assigns.

> **Playbook Note:** Perpetual confidentiality on all information exceeds playbook standard (3–5 years for non-trade-secret information). Binding heirs and "future pets" is not a cognizable legal obligation and is struck.

---

### 4. ~~Liquidated Damages~~ **[REPLACE: Limitation of Liability]**

~~The Receiving Party acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement will cause incalculable and catastrophic harm to the Disclosing Party. Therefore, in the event of a breach or a suspected breach, the Receiving Party agrees to immediately pay the Disclosing Party liquidated damages in the amount of **$10,000,000.00 (Ten Million Dollars)** per syllable disclosed, without the Disclosing Party needing to prove actual damages.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** Each party's aggregate liability to the other for any breach of this Agreement shall not exceed **$5,000,000 (Five Million Dollars)**. Nothing in this Section shall limit either party's liability for damages arising from gross negligence or willful misconduct. The Disclosing Party must demonstrate actual damages in any action for breach.

> **Playbook Note:** "$10M per syllable" is punitive and unenforceable. Playbook requires a defined cap; because this is a confidentiality agreement, a super-cap of $5M is applied consistent with the playbook exception for breaches of confidentiality.

---

### 5. ~~Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation~~ **[DELETED IN FULL]**

~~By signing this NDA, the Receiving Party agrees that for a period of fifty (50) years following any interaction with the Disclosing Party, they will not work for, consult with, or look directly at any other company that operates on the planet Earth.~~

**[DELETE ENTIRELY.]**

> **Playbook Note:** A 50-year global non-compete is not an NDA provision. It is an unenforceable restraint of trade in virtually every jurisdiction. This section is struck with no replacement. If the parties wish to negotiate a narrowly tailored non-solicitation, that should be addressed in a separate commercial agreement.

---

### 6. Governing Law: ~~Texas~~ **[AMENDED: New York]**

~~**Governing Law: Texas.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Furthermore, both parties agree that the exclusive venue for any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be the state courts located in **Loving County, Texas** (population 51). The Receiving Party expressly waives any right to claim this is an inconvenient forum, and agrees to travel there by horse or buggy if necessary.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of New York**, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Each party hereby submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in **New York County, New York** for resolution of any dispute arising under this Agreement, and waives any objection to the convenience of such forum.

> **Playbook Note:** Texas is not on the approved governing law list (NY, DE, CA). Loving County venue was a bad-faith drafting choice designed to make litigation impractical. Replaced with New York.

---

### 7. ~~Attorney's Fees and Firstborn Clause~~ **[AMENDED: Attorney's Fees]**

~~In the event of any litigation arising from this Agreement, the Receiving Party agrees to pay 100% of the Disclosing Party's attorney's fees, travel expenses, and luxury hotel accommodations, regardless of who actually wins the lawsuit.~~

**[REPLACE WITH:]** In any action to enforce this Agreement, the **prevailing party** shall be entitled to recover its reasonable and documented attorney's fees and costs from the non-prevailing party.

> **Playbook Note:** One-sided fee shifting regardless of outcome is commercially unreasonable. Replaced with standard prevailing-party language. "Luxury hotel accommodations" struck — not a recoverable litigation cost.
